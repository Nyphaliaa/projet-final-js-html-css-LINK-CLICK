SYNOPSIS

Upon meeting Lu Guang, Cheng Xiaoshi discovers that they share unique abilities. Combining their powers, they work together to rescue the ailing Time Photo Studio by fulfilling clients' photographic desires. Unfortunately, their harmonious existence is disrupted by the emergence of a perplexing murderer that jeopardizes the safety of Lu Guang. Meanwhile, Qiao Ling becomes embroiled in a hazardous scheme. As these hindrances arise, how will Cheng Xiaoshi navigate these perilous circumstances?
